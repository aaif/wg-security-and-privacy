# Taxonomy of Terms and Definitions

> **Status:** Moved to the cross-WG Taxonomy and Landscape workstream
> **Last Modified:** 2026-06-22

The Taxonomy of Terms and Definitions is now maintained by the cross-WG **Taxonomy and Landscape** workstream. This is an AAIF-wide effort that builds common terminology and resolves conflicting definitions across every working group.

## Where the work lives now

The taxonomy is developed in the workstream's repository: [aaif/ws-taxonomy-landscape](https://github.com/aaif/ws-taxonomy-landscape). Contributions happen there through pull requests. The workstream holds a weekly 30-minute sync and does most of its work asynchronously.

## How the Security and Privacy WG participates

The Security and Privacy WG contributed its original taxonomy draft as a starting point for the cross-WG effort. The WG stays involved through delegates who review terminology and help resolve definition questions on its behalf:

- Jay White
- Katherine Druckman

The taxonomy began inside this working group and was elevated to a cross-WG AAIF initiative ([2026-05-12](../meeting-notes/2026-05-12.md)). Junjie Bu was appointed to lead it ([2026-05-26](../meeting-notes/2026-05-26.md)), and the WG named its delegates on [2026-06-09](../meeting-notes/2026-06-09.md).
